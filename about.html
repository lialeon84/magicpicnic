About Us
Our vision
We want you and your loved ones, friends, and colleagues to enjoy quality time together. Relax and connect with each other while getting away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life to enjoy the beautiful ambience and closeness with nature.
What we do
We specialize in creating picture-perfect picnics with indulgent grazing platters. Allow us the pleasure of coordinating, styling, and doing all the work while you focus on enjoying the moment and making long-lasting.
What we stand for
To us, it's more than just a simple picnic. It's an experience to enjoy to the fullest. We use authentic design pieces collected from all over the world to create a unique ambiance and our grazing platters only use high-quality ingredients.
Hi there! Thanks for stopping by Magic Picnic. I'm Zuly; born abroad, lover of travel, and now settled down in beautiful Miami, Fl. I was always a wanderer, one who loved to escape city life at every chance; to breathe in nature's beauty while enjoying good company and great food. When you put that all together, it pretty much sums up what sparked my passion for offering unique and intimate picnic experiences.
My goal is to create something that allows people to experience nature in a seamless way, with great quality, comfort, and pleasing aesthetics. With this philosophy in mind, I founded Magic Picnic, which offers picnics with a unique bohemian style inspired by travel to far off places. I want to give people the opportunity to slow down, unwind, and spend quality time with the people they care about.
